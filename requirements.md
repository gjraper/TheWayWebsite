# Website Requirements: The Way Praise and Worship

## Project Overview
The goal is to create a modern, premium static website for "The Way Praise and Worship". The website will serve as a digital front door for the community, providing essential information about service times, social media presence, and contact details. The site will be optimized for performance and deployed on Vercel.

## Core Features

### 1. Title Page (Hero Section)
- **Objective**: Create a welcoming and impactful first impression.
- **Requirements**:
    - High-quality background image or video (using `generate_image` or placeholders).
    - Clear value proposition or mission statement.
    - Strong Call to Action (CTA) like "Join Us This Sunday".
    - Navigation menu (Home, About, Sermons, Contact).

### 2. Sermon Times Section
- **Objective**: Inform visitors when services are held.
- **Requirements**:
    - Clearly legible list of service times (e.g., Sunday 9:00 AM & 11:00 AM).
    - Location information (Address with Google Maps link).
    - "Add to Calendar" functionality (optional but recommended for premium feel).

### 3. Social Media Links
- **Objective**: Connect the community with the church's digital content.
- **Requirements**:
    - Dedicated section or footer icons for **Facebook** and **YouTube**.
    - Live stream alerts or links to past sermons.

### 4. Contact Us Section
- **Objective**: Provide a way for visitors to reach out.
- **Requirements**:
    - Contact form (Name, Email, Message).
    - Direct contact info (Phone, Email, Physical Address).
    - Interactive map integration.

## Design & Aesthetics
- **Style**: Premium, modern, and clean.
- **Aesthetics**: Use rich gradients, modern typography (e.g., Inter/Outfit), and subtle micro-animations.
- **Responsiveness**: Fully responsive design (Mobile, Tablet, Desktop).
- **Accessibility**: Compliance with basic accessibility standards (alt text, high contrast).

## Technical Requirements
- **Framework**: Next.js (recommended for Vercel deployment) or Vite.
- **Styling**: Vanilla CSS with a structured design system.
- **Deployment**: Vercel (Automatic CI/CD).
- **SEO**: Meta tags, descriptive title, and semantic HTML structure.
