About Us

Discover our journey and the heart behind our community.

Our Beliefs

Every community begins with a single step of faith. The Way Praise and Worship was founded on the belief that faith should be accessible, community should be authentic, and worship should be vibrant.

Our faith is centered around five core truths:

01

Life is amazing.

02

God loves you in an amazing and extravagant way.

03

Everyday, someone is praying for you.

04

Every great opportunity, in this life, is still available to you.

05

Your best life begins with your current life.

We are a nondenominational church drawing inspiration from Methodist, Baptist, and Pentecostal beliefs. You need only bring what you have if you wish to join us in the pursuit of living your best life.

Our Story

The Way Praise and Worship began as a vision to create a space that feels like home, yet challenges the heart to grow. Our journey is one of community, connection, and a shared pursuit of truth in modern life. As we continue to write our history, we invite you to be part of the chapters yet to come.

"He leads us to live life at the razor's edge of our capabilities, striving for growth and achievement."

Anthony, our pastor, began teaching in 1997. As an Austinite and UT Alum, he cultivates purpose and belonging with our congregation in this city.

He is a devout believer in prayer and the power it holds in our lives, and he's excited for the opportunity to pray with you.
