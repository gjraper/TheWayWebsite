Our Story

Every community begins with a single step of faith. The Way Praise and Worship was founded on the belief that faith should be accessible, community should be authentic, and worship should be vibrant.

Our story is one of transformation and growth. What started as a small gathering of dedicated individuals has evolved into a thriving community of people from all walks of life. We are united by a common desire to explore truth, express gratitude, and support one another in our spiritual journeys.

At the heart of our story is a commitment to radical hospitality. We believe that everyone—regardless of their background or where they are in their faith journey—deserves a seat at the table. We are a family that celebrates together, mourns together, and walks together.

As we look to the future, we remain dedicated to our core values while finding new and creative ways to serve our city and the world. Whether it's through our Sunday services, community outreach, or online presence, our goal remains the same: to provide a place where you can find truth and walk together with us.
